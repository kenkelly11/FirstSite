Welcome to Cafe Amarillo, the coffee shop-stop for all your caffeine and catering needs! The idea for a yellow-themed cafe started in my fiance's hometown of Key West, where we were lucky enough to set up and settle down. Our mutual love of the color yellow paired with pride for his Cuban ancestry brought us to the name and theme Amarillo. Stop in today to get your hands on one of our signature yellow lattes with a flavoring of your choice. Or, if coffee isn't for you, try any one of our made-fresh-every-morning bakery items! If you're coming in on your lunch break, we've got just the thing to satisfy your hunger pangs: sandwiches, soups, salads, and more!
